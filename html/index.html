Peerly

Peerly is a mobile application that allows students to find peers who are looking to be tutors, who are looking for tutors, or are just looking for a study partner. They are matched with other users based off the classes they are taking or classes they have completed. This applications was made using React Native and Expo Go with a team of 3 developers.
